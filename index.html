About Me

I am a machine learning and AI practitioner at Carnegie Mellon University, where I build applied AI systems that create real-world impact. I currently work with Prof. Anand Rao on research exploring the future of work, developing frameworks to evaluate human-AI teaming and simulate workforce policy scenarios.

In parallel, I'm building Cuddle Code, an AI-powered assessment platform designed to help organizations identify problem-solvers who can thrive in AI-augmented workplaces—a serendipitous alignment with my research interests.

Previously, I was a Data Engineer at Fractal Analytics, where I built MLOps pipelines and data products for Fortune 500 clients in transportation and retail.

I love bridging the gap between research and industry, turning ideas into solutions that deliver meaningful impact.
